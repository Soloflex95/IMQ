# IMQ System Features

## 1. Qualification Management

- Capture and manage worker qualifications:
  - Work experience
  - Education
  - Certifications
  - Skills
- Structured data entry (no free-form CV dependency)
- Centralized qualification records per worker

---

## 2. Job Requirement Management

- Define job roles and associated requirements
- Configure:
  - Required skills
  - Required education
  - Required certifications
- Reusable requirement templates
- Version-controlled job requirements

---

## 3. Automated Qualification Analysis Engine

- Compare worker qualifications against job requirements
- Identify:
  - Matches
  - Gaps (deficiencies)
- Provide real-time qualification status:
  - Qualified
  - Not Qualified
  - Pending

---

## 4. Assessment and Approval Workflow

- Manager-driven assessment process
- Review system-generated analysis
- Apply decision:
  - Qualified
  - Not Qualified
  - Further Review Required
- Apply electronic signature for final approval
- Store finalized assessment with audit traceability

---

## 5. Audit Trail and Compliance Tracking

- Automatic logging of all key actions:
  - Create
  - Update
  - Delete
  - Approve
- Immutable audit records
- Full traceability of user actions

---

## 6. Audit and Inspection Readiness

- Generate audit-ready reports on demand
- Publish controlled reports for auditors
- Time-restricted access for external reviewers
- Rapid retrieval of qualification evidence

---

## 7. Role-Based User Experience

- Role-specific interfaces:
  - Worker
  - Manager
  - Administrator
  - Auditor
- Simplified workflows per role
- Controlled access to features and data

---

## 8. Notifications and Alerts

- Notify managers of:
  - Qualification gaps
  - Pending assessments
  - Periodic review requirements
- Trigger alerts based on:
  - Time intervals
  - Role changes
  - Requirement updates

---

## 9. Periodic Review and Compliance Monitoring

- Automated periodic review scheduling
- Manager re-evaluation workflows
- Continuous compliance monitoring
- Updated audit records for each review

---

## 10. Data Security and Privacy Controls

- Tenant-based data segregation
- Secure storage of personal data
- Controlled access to sensitive information
- Compliance with data privacy regulations

---

## 11. Integration Capabilities

- API-based integration with:
  - HR systems
  - Learning Management Systems (LMS)
  - Training systems
- Ability to import/export qualification data
- Support for future external system integrations

---

## 12. Reporting and Analytics

- Dashboard views for:
  - Qualification status
  - Compliance levels
  - Team readiness
- Exportable reports
- Insights into workforce qualification gaps
